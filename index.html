Whitecroft Gardens unveils a rare collection of seven exquisite residences that redefine luxury living.

Follow the progress on Instagram

Each bedroom has been designed with the utmost attention to detail, featuring handpicked products and elegant sanctuary spaces complemented by a curated colour palette from the Empire Osea® collection. Created to pay homage to Milan, one of Italy's most beautiful cities and a hub of fashion and design, where every street, square, and building evokes beauty and history, the Empire Osea® collection is inspired by Milan's premium pre-fall porter fashion and harmoniously blends design and functionality.

Most bathrooms include Laufen vanities, while primary suites boast bespoke dual sink vanity units. Fully tiled, marble-inspired primary bathrooms merge convenience with elegance. Detached homes also enjoy the added luxury of freestanding baths and separate showers, creating tranquil retreats. Additionally, each home features a downstairs guest WC, enhancing functionality and comfort.

Step into the epitome of rest and relaxation, where each bedroom has been designed to maximise comfort and luxury. Primary suites boast spacious walk-in dressing areas and en-suite bathrooms, enhancing both convenience and sophistication. Each primary bedroom is adorned with bespoke wardrobes and selected bedrooms have special accents in such as bespoke wallpaper and fluted panelled walls, complemented by plush, soft carpeting.

Whitecroft Gardens enjoys a coveted position in the heart of the Southborough, picturesque Kentish village surrounded by rolling countryside and mature woodlands. With excellent rail connections, London commuters can reach the city in less than an hour, seamlessly blending the best of rural tranquillity with urban accessibility.
